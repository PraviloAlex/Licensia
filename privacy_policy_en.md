# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 29, 2026**

---

## 1. Who we are

Licencia AR is an independent study tool to help users prepare for the theoretical driving license exam. We are not an official application of the Gobierno de la Ciudad de Buenos Aires, the Agencia Nacional de Seguridad Vial, or any other government body.

---

## 2. Data

**What we store.** The app may save locally on your device your study progress, language preference, visual theme, text size, and usage settings. This data is used solely to ensure the app functions correctly.

**What we do not collect.** We do not request or collect user accounts, names, emails, phone numbers, ID documents, addresses, location, or any other personal information. Payment data is also never received or stored by us — it is processed directly by Google Play.

---

## 3. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

The app does not use advertising, analytics, tracking, user accounts, or cloud synchronization. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

---

## 4. Local storage and data retention

Study progress, answers, mistakes, vocabulary state, and settings are stored locally on your device. Data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data through your browser or device settings. We have no access to this data.

---

## 5. Cache and offline mode

The PWA version of the app may cache files to improve loading speed and enable offline use. The cache is stored locally and can be cleared through your browser settings.

---

## 6. External links

The app may contain links to official sources and public materials. We do not control the content or privacy policies of external websites and are not responsible for them.

---

## 7. Minors

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you become aware that a minor has provided personal data, please contact us.

---

## 8. User rights

Since all data is stored exclusively on your device, you can access, modify, or delete it at any time through your browser or device settings — no request to us is needed.

If you have questions or requests related to your data, you can reach us at the contact address below.

---

## 9. Purchases and subscriptions

The app offers paid features through one-time purchases and/or subscriptions available via Google Play. All payments are processed by Google Play — we do not receive, see, or store your card details or payment account information.

Subscription management (pause, cancellation) is handled through Google Play settings on your device. After cancellation, access to paid features remains available until the end of the paid period. Refunds are governed by the [Google Play refund policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637).

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. For significant changes, the date at the top of the document will be updated. We recommend checking the current version periodically.
